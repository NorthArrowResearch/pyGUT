### Unit Shape
- **Convexity**
- **Planar**
- **Concavity**

### Unit Form
- **Mound** - Convex laterally and longitudinally
- **Saddle** - Convex longitudinally
- **Wall** - Steep and flat 
- **Plane** - Flat (i.e., neither convex nor concave)
- **Trough** - Concave laterally but not longitudinally
- **Bowl** - Concave laterally and longitudinally
